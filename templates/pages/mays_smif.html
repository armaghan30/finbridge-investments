About MAYS SMIF

The MAYS Student Managed Investment Fund is a first-of-its-kind initiative designed to give students hands-on experience in portfolio management, fundamental analysis, and real-world investing on the Pakistan Stock Exchange.

Through this program, students learn to analyze PSX-listed companies, make investment decisions, manage risk, and experience the full lifecycle of investment management.

Portfolio Management

Students manage a real portfolio of PSX stocks with guidance from industry professionals.

Fundamental Analysis

Learn to evaluate companies through financial statements, ratios, and qualitative factors.

Team Collaboration

Work in teams to research, pitch investment ideas, and make collective decisions.

Industry Exposure

Connect with finance professionals, fund managers, and industry leaders.
