FinBridge Scholars Program

Recognizing analytical excellence and building future leaders in Pakistan's capital markets

Program Overview

As part of our commitment to advancing financial education and fostering young investment talent, FinBridge has established the FinBridge Scholars Program, a performance-based scholarship awarded to exceptional participants of the MAYS Student-Managed Investment Fund (SMIF). The program is designed to recognize analytical excellence, promote ethical investing, and build a pipeline of future leaders who will contribute to a more transparent and progressive financial ecosystem in Pakistan.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be enrolled in a bachelor's degree in finance, Accounting & Finance, or a closely related discipline at a recognized university.
  • Be active participants of the Student-Managed Investment Fund (SMIF).
  • Demonstrate consistent performance in portfolio analysis, asset allocation, and risk management.
  • Exhibit strong ethical standards, teamwork, and leadership within the fund.
  • Maintain a satisfactory academic standing and commitment to professional development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct in-depth equity research and maintain detailed financial models for assigned sectors.
  • Participate in portfolio strategy discussions and contribute to fund decision-making.
  • Prepare investment reports and presentations for review by FinBridge leadership.
  • Uphold the principles of responsible investing, transparency, and long-term value creation.

Awards & Recognition

  • A financial scholarship recognizing academic and professional merit.
  • A Certificate of Distinction endorsed by FinBridge.
  • Mentorship opportunities with experienced professionals in finance and investment.
  • Potential preference for internship or employment opportunities within FinBridge or its affiliated entities.

Program Objective

Through this initiative, FinBridge aims to bridge academic knowledge with practical investment experience, nurture analytical talent, and contribute to the development of future leaders in Pakistan's capital markets.
